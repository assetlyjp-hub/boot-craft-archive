## Overview

The John Lofgren Engineer is a engineer from john-lofgren, made in Japan. Priced at $900, it features chromexcel leather with goodyear welt construction.

The John Lofgren Engineer boot is a meticulous Japanese reproduction of the classic American engineer boot. Handcrafted in Japan using Horween Chromexcel leather and vintage manufacturing techniques, it represents the pinnacle of the 'better than the original' philosophy that Japanese bootmakers are renowned for. Every detail, from the brass buckle to the leather-lined interior, is executed to perfection.

## Construction & Materials

- **Leather**: Chromexcel
- **Construction**: Goodyear Welt
- **Sole**: Vibram
- **Last Shape**: Munson
- **Made In**: Japan

## Key Features

- Horween CXL leather upper
- Goodyear welt construction
- Vibram 700 sole
- Steel shank reinforcement
- Handmade in Japan with vintage techniques

## Fit & Sizing

As with most goodyear welt boots, we recommend trying the John Lofgren Engineer in your Brannock size and potentially sizing down by half a size. The Munson last provides a balanced fit that accommodates most foot shapes.

## Value Assessment

At $900, the John Lofgren Engineer sits in the luxury price range. For a goodyear welt boot made in Japan with chromexcel leather, this represents solid value in today's market.

## Verdict

The John Lofgren Engineer is a worthy addition to any footwear collection. john-lofgren's commitment to quality materials and proven construction methods ensures this is a boot that will serve you well for years to come.
