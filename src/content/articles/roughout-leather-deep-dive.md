## What Is Roughout?

Roughout leather is conventional leather turned inside out, exposing the flesh side. Originally used in military boots for its resistance to scuffs and abrasion, roughout develops a rugged character over time. It hides scratches naturally and requires minimal maintenance compared to smooth leathers.

## Care Recommendations

To maintain Roughout leather:

1. Brush regularly with a horsehair brush after each wear
2. Condition every 4-8 weeks depending on wear frequency
3. Use shoe trees to maintain shape and absorb moisture
4. Avoid direct heat sources when drying
