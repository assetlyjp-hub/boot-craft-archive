## What Is Chromexcel?

Chromexcel is Horween's legendary pull-up leather, tanned using a proprietary 89-step combination tanning process involving chrome, vegetable tannins, and hot stuffing with natural oils and greases. Known for its rich depth of color, self-healing scratch resistance, and exceptional patina development over time.

## Properties at a Glance

- **Durability**: 4/5
- **Water Resistance**: 3/5
- **Patina Development**: 5/5
- **Suppleness**: 5/5
- **Ease of Maintenance**: 3/5

## Tanning Process

Chromexcel is produced using combination tanning methods by Horween. This process gives the leather its distinctive character and determines how it ages over time.

## Care Recommendations

To maintain Chromexcel leather:

1. Brush regularly with a horsehair brush after each wear
2. Condition every 4-8 weeks depending on wear frequency
3. Use shoe trees to maintain shape and absorb moisture
4. Avoid direct heat sources when drying

## Best Boots in Chromexcel

Chromexcel is popular among heritage boot makers for its unique combination of properties. Look for offerings from brands that source from Horween for the highest quality specimens.

## Conclusion

Chromexcel remains one of the most beloved leathers in the footwear world. Whether you prioritize patina, durability, or ease of care, understanding this leather will help you make a more informed purchase decision.
