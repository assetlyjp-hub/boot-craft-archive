## Overview

The Carmina Shell Cordovan Derby is a derby from Carmina, made in Spain. Priced at $750, it features shell-cordovan leather with goodyear welt construction.

Carmina's Shell Cordovan Derby represents exceptional European craftsmanship. Made in their Mallorca workshop using Horween Shell Cordovan, the Rain last provides a sleek yet comfortable fit at a price well below English and American competitors.

## Construction & Materials

- **Leather**: Shell Cordovan
- **Construction**: Goodyear Welt
- **Sole**: Leather
- **Last Shape**: Rain
- **Made In**: Spain

## Key Features

- Horween Shell Cordovan
- Rain last
- Blake/Rapid available
- Handmade in Mallorca

## Fit & Sizing

As with most goodyear welt boots, we recommend trying the Carmina Shell Cordovan Derby in your Brannock size and potentially sizing down by half a size. The Rain last provides a balanced fit that accommodates most foot shapes.

## Value Assessment

At $750, the Carmina Shell Cordovan Derby sits in the luxury price range. For a goodyear welt boot made in Spain with shell-cordovan leather, this represents solid value in today's market.

## Verdict

The Carmina Shell Cordovan Derby is a worthy addition to any footwear collection. Carmina's commitment to quality materials and proven construction methods ensures this is a boot that will serve you well for years to come.
