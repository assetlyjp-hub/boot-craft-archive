## Overview

The Grant Stone Ottawa Boot is a service boot from Grant Stone, made in China. Priced at $380, it features roughout leather with stitchdown construction.

The Ottawa Boot uses stitchdown construction for a wider, more rugged aesthetic. Roughout leather resists scuffs and develops a unique character over time. A great alternative to Red Wing's moc toe line.

## Construction & Materials

- **Leather**: Roughout
- **Construction**: Stitchdown
- **Sole**: Rubber
- **Last Shape**: Leo
- **Made In**: China

## Key Features

- Roughout leather upper
- Stitchdown construction
- Vibram Morflex sole
- Moc toe silhouette

## Fit & Sizing

As with most stitchdown boots, we recommend trying the Grant Stone Ottawa Boot in your Brannock size and potentially sizing down by half a size. The Leo last provides a balanced fit that accommodates most foot shapes.

## Value Assessment

At $380, the Grant Stone Ottawa Boot sits in the mid price range. For a stitchdown boot made in China with roughout leather, this represents solid value in today's market.

## Verdict

The Grant Stone Ottawa Boot is a worthy addition to any footwear collection. Grant Stone's commitment to quality materials and proven construction methods ensures this is a boot that will serve you well for years to come.
