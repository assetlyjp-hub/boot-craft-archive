## What Is Shell Cordovan?

Shell Cordovan is the most prized leather in footwear, made from the oval shell membrane beneath a horse's hindquarters. Horween's vegetable tanning process takes six months to complete. The resulting leather develops a mirror-like patina, resists creasing, and can last a lifetime with proper care.

## Properties at a Glance

- **Durability**: 5/5
- **Water Resistance**: 2/5
- **Patina Development**: 5/5
- **Suppleness**: 3/5
- **Ease of Maintenance**: 2/5

## Tanning Process

Shell Cordovan is produced using vegetable tanning methods by Horween. This process gives the leather its distinctive character and determines how it ages over time.

## Care Recommendations

To maintain Shell Cordovan leather:

1. Brush regularly with a horsehair brush after each wear
2. Condition every 4-8 weeks depending on wear frequency
3. Use shoe trees to maintain shape and absorb moisture
4. Avoid direct heat sources when drying

## Best Boots in Shell Cordovan

Shell Cordovan is popular among heritage boot makers for its unique combination of properties. Look for offerings from brands that source from Horween for the highest quality specimens.

## Conclusion

Shell Cordovan remains one of the most beloved leathers in the footwear world. Whether you prioritize patina, durability, or ease of care, understanding this leather will help you make a more informed purchase decision.
