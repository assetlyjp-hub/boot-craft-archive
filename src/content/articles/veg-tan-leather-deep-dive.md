## What Is Vegetable Tanned?

Vegetable tanned leather uses natural tannins from tree bark and plants in a process that can take weeks to months. The result is a firm, characterful leather that develops the most dramatic patina of any leather type. It stiffens and molds to the wearer's foot over time, creating a truly personal fit.

## Tanning Process

Vegetable Tanned is produced using vegetable tanning methods by Various. This process gives the leather its distinctive character and determines how it ages over time.

## Care Recommendations

To maintain Vegetable Tanned leather:

1. Brush regularly with a horsehair brush after each wear
2. Condition every 4-8 weeks depending on wear frequency
3. Use shoe trees to maintain shape and absorb moisture
4. Avoid direct heat sources when drying
