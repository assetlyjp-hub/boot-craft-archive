## Overview

The Paraboot Michael is a derby from Paraboot, made in France. Priced at $530, it features veg-tan leather with norwegian welt construction.

The Michael is Paraboot's take on the Tyrolean mountain shoe. Its chunky double-stitched Norwegian welt and thick rubber sole provide unmatched weather protection. A favorite of architects, designers, and those who value function as much as form.

## Construction & Materials

- **Leather**: Veg Tan
- **Construction**: Norwegian Welt
- **Sole**: Rubber
- **Last Shape**: Michael
- **Made In**: France

## Key Features

- Norwegian welt construction
- Tyrolean-style design
- Marche rubber sole
- Double-stitched construction

## Fit & Sizing

As with most norwegian welt boots, we recommend trying the Paraboot Michael in your Brannock size and potentially sizing down by half a size. The Michael last provides a balanced fit that accommodates most foot shapes.

## Value Assessment

At $530, the Paraboot Michael sits in the premium price range. For a norwegian welt boot made in France with veg-tan leather, this represents solid value in today's market.

## Verdict

The Paraboot Michael is a worthy addition to any footwear collection. Paraboot's commitment to quality materials and proven construction methods ensures this is a boot that will serve you well for years to come.
